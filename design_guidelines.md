## Design Approach
**Reference-Based Approach**: Drawing inspiration from high-converting e-commerce landing pages (Shopify product pages, Amazon product listings) combined with modern pet care brand aesthetics. ## Core Design Elements

### Typography
- **Primary Headline Font**: Bold, friendly sans-serif (700-800 weight) for emotional impact
- **Body Text**: Clean, readable sans-serif (400 weight) 
- **Accent Text**: Medium weight (500-600) for CTAs and highlights
- **Hierarchy**: Large hero headlines (3xl-4xl), medium section headers (2xl-3xl), readable body text (base-lg)

### Color Palette (User-Specified)
- **Primary**: Blue-light tones (cleanliness, trust)
- **Secondary**: White (clean background)
- **Accent**: Mint green (nature, wellness)
- **Supporting**: Warm tones for urgency elements (stock counter)

### Layout System
**Spacing**: Tailwind units of 4, 6, 8, 12, 16, 20 for consistent rhythm
- Section padding: py-12 to py-20
- Container: max-w-6xl for content sections
- Grid gaps: gap-6 to gap-8
- Mobile-first responsive approach
